## What is Life Tracker

**Life Tracker** is a free Progressive Web App for personal health monitoring. Log your weight, sleep, calories, steps, vital parameters, meals, and medications in one app — accessible from any device with no installation required.

Every piece of data you enter is automatically analyzed to provide personalized insights, correlations between your habits, and concrete suggestions to improve your well-being.

---

## Why it's useful

- **Everything in one place** — no more switching between separate apps for weight, sleep, calories, and steps
- **Automatic insights** — the app analyzes your data and shows you patterns and correlations you wouldn't spot on your own
- **Clear charts** — visualize trends over time with interactive charts filterable by week, month, or year
- **Concrete goals** — set a target weight, daily calories, steps, and sleep hours, then track your progress
- **Google Fit sync** — data from your phone arrives automatically, no need to re-enter it
- **Works everywhere** — on smartphones, tablets, and desktops, even offline

## How to use it

### 1. Sign up

Go to [personal-health-by-bonn.web.app](https://personal-health-by-bonn.web.app), create an account with email and password, and verify your email.

### 2. Complete your profile

Enter your name, date of birth, gender, physical activity level, and target weight. The app automatically calculates your daily caloric needs (TDEE).

### 3. Set your goals

Define your target weight, daily calorie goal, step target, and ideal sleep hours. The app uses these values to color-code charts and give you real-time feedback.

### 4. Start logging your data

Every day you can record:
- Body weight
- Sleep hours (with an hours/minutes picker or precise bedtime and wake-up times)
- Calories consumed
- Steps taken
- Blood pressure, heart rate, glucose
- Water, coffee, alcohol, cigarettes
- Meals in the food diary
- Medications taken

### 5. Connect Google Fit (optional)

From the settings section, connect your Google Fit account. From that point on, weight, steps, calories, sleep, glucose, blood pressure, heart rate, and workouts will sync automatically in both directions.

### 6. Read your insights

The app automatically analyzes your data and shows you personalized suggestions, correlations between your habits, and comparisons with previous periods.

## Key Features

### Body weight
Complete history with trend chart, monthly calendar with color coding (green = improvement toward goal, red = moving away from it), BMI calculation, and target weight.

### Advanced sleep
Log hours slept with an intuitive hours/minutes picker or by entering the precise times you fell asleep and woke up. The insight system analyzes your sleep across **7 categories**: schedule patterns, perceived quality, consistency, correlations with other data (e.g., weight and calories), personalized recommendations, trends over time, and weekly comparisons.

### Vital parameters
Blood pressure, heart rate, and glucose with dedicated charts and complete history. Tap each point on the chart to see the measurement detail.

### Calories and TDEE
Automatic calculation of caloric needs based on your profile. Daily tracking with insights on caloric surplus and deficit.

### Meal plan and food diary
Build your weekly plan with alternatives for breakfast, snacks, lunch, and dinner. Log in the diary what you actually eat, with weight, quantity, and calories for each meal.

### Detailed diet
Daily counters for water, coffee, alcohol, and cigarettes with weekly and monthly statistics.

### Physical activity
Daily steps with a personalized goal, custom workout plans, and a completed workouts log.

### Medications
Medication reminders with automatic renewal every 3 months — never forget to restock your medicines again.

### Smart insights
The analysis engine identifies patterns in your habits, discovers correlations between different data points (e.g., "you sleep better on days when you walk more"), and compares different weeks and months.

### Google Fit
Two-way sync of weight, steps, calories, sleep, glucose, blood pressure, heart rate, and completed workouts.

### Telegram notifications
Connect your Telegram account to receive a weekly and monthly progress report.

### Customization
Choose which sections to show and in what order — hide the ones you don't use and rearrange tabs however you like.

### CSV export
Export all your data in CSV format to always have a complete backup.

### Multilingual
Interface available in Italian and English with instant switching — all messages and labels are translated in real time.

### PWA — Installable and offline
Add Life Tracker to your phone's home screen just like a native app. Works even without an internet connection.

## Technologies

Built with **vanilla JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3** (no frontend framework). Backend on **Firebase** (Firestore, Authentication, Cloud Functions, Hosting). Charts with **Chart.js**. Fitness sync via **Google Fit API**. **PWA** architecture with Service Worker for offline support.
